Online Resale Mela

Project Overview

The Online Resale Mela is a dynamic web-based platform for the buying and selling of pre-owned vehicles. The primary goal of the platform is to create a seamless marketplace for users to list, discover, and purchase used vehicles. With a user-friendly interface and robust backend systems, the project ensures secure and efficient transactions while simplifying the resale process for vehicles.
Secure user registration and login using role-based authentication for buyers and sellers. Profile management to track listings, purchases, and interactions. Sellers can post vehicles for sale with essential details such as model, brand, year, mileage, condition, and price.Upload images and additional descriptions to attract buyers.
